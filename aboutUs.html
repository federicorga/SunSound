Who We Are:

At the heart of the SunSound Community, we find ourselves as a passionate group of independent artists: DJs and producers, programmers and NFT artists. We come together with the vision of transcending conventional boundaries, fusing digital art with music to create experiences that are not only heard, but felt. Our Mission: Connect Digital Art with Music We seek to break barriers by fusing digital art with music, transmitting unique emotions through the synthesis of both worlds. We believe in the ability of NFT art to amplify and bring electronic music to life, creating a unique synergy that redefines the artistic experience. Core Values: Co-Participation and Mutual Growth At SunSound Community, we are guided by fundamental values ​​that strengthen the essence of our community. We believe in co-participation between artists, fostering an unmatched chemistry that gives rise to unique art. We emphasize mutual growth, providing our members with fair economic recognition and exposure.

Recognizing the Value of the Artist:

We understand that industries often exploit artistic talent to generate huge sums of money, while artists barely make a fraction of the profits. At SunSound Community, we consider it vital to correct this disparity. We strive to give artists the recognition and financial compensation they deserve for their unique contribution to the world of art and electronic music. Focusing on mutual growth and fair valuation, we aim to create a space where independent artists thrive and shine. Join us at SunSound Community, where the connection between digital art and music is more than artistic expression; It's a revolution.

  1. Transparency and Decentralization:

    The blockchain is a distributed ledger that is decentralized. Every action, from art creation to token transactions, is recorded transparently and accessible to all participants.

  2. Smart Contracts:

    We use smart contracts, which are autonomous programs on the blockchain. These contracts automatically execute predefined agreements when certain conditions are met, ensuring reliable execution of established terms.

  3. Authenticity and Intellectual Property:

    Each digital artwork is represented as an NFT token on the blockchain. These NFTs not only serve as proof of authenticity, but they also establish intellectual property, ensuring that artists receive the recognition and royalties they deserve every time their work is used or sold.

  4. Native Token:

    We integrate a native token into our platform, which not only serves as a medium of exchange, but also grants exclusive access to electronic music events organized by SunSound Community. This token is based on blockchain standards and is backed by the strength of technology to ensure its security and functionality.

  5. Financial Empowerment:

    Blockchain facilitates direct transactions between artists and their audiences, eliminating middlemen and allowing artists to obtain fairer compensation for their work. This solves the common problem in the industry, where artists often see a tiny fraction of the profits generated by their art.

  6. Active Community:

    Our blockchain-based platform encourages active community participation. Members can vote on key decisions, propose changes and contribute to the growth and development of the SunSound Community. Together, SunSound Community's blockchain technology not only drives transparency and security, but redefines the relationship between artists and their audience, enabling a new paradigm where digital art and electronic music converge in a revolutionary way. We are building a future where creativity flourishes, and blockchain is the tool that makes it possible.
