Community

Welcome to SunSound Community, a revolutionary space designed to transform the experience of electronic music artists globally. Our community is founded on the belief that artists deserve the artistic and economic recognition that truly belongs to them.

Purpose:

At SunSound Community, our purpose is to create a platform that connects electronic music artists from around the world. Through blockchain technology and the issuance of art NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens), we seek to empower artists, allowing them to obtain maximum profitability for their art and set a new standard for fair compensation.

/div>

Mission:

The mission of SunSound Community is clear: we want artists to be recognized as they deserve in the electronic music industry. Through our platform, we aim to provide artists with the tools and visibility necessary to excel in a digital world. We seek to not only transform the way artists are compensated, but also change the overall perception of their importance within the industry. Join the SunSound Community as we work toward a future where artists thrive, electronic music is celebrated, and blockchain technology redefines the way we experience music in unique and unforgettable events.
